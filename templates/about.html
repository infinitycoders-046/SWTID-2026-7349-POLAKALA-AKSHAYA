About This Project

Behind the Harvest Data

An academic deep-dive into India's agricultural output, combining open government datasets with Tableau's visual storytelling power.

📋 Project Overview

This project analyses India's agricultural crop production from 1997 to 2021, covering food grains, oilseeds, pulses, commercial crops, and horticulture. Using Tableau, we built interactive dashboards that let users explore production volume, area harvested, and yield per hectare at the state and national level.

The goal is to surface patterns that inform policy, research, and public understanding of food security trends in the world's most populous nation.

🗃️ Dataset Details
  • Source: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare (MoAFW)
  • Secondary: data.gov.in open data portal
  • Time span: 1997–98 to 2022–23 crop years
  • Coverage: 28 states + 8 Union Territories
  • Crops: 55 crop types across kharif & rabi seasons
  • Metrics: Production (MT), Area (Mha), Yield (Kg/Ha)
  • Format: CSV / Excel, cleaned in Python (pandas)
